Topic 3: 해체

Casual / Intimate Speech

When to Use?

  • With close friends and best friends
  • Talking to younger siblings or family members
  • Classmates of the same age
  • People you are very close and comfortable with
  • Text messaging with friends
  • Online chatting in informal settings
  • Warning: DO NOT use with strangers or superiors
⚠️ Important: Using 해체 with strangers, elders, or superiors can sound very rude and disrespectful. Only use with people you have close relationships with.

Examples in Context

Statement
나 학교 가.
I'm going to school.
Question
밥 먹었어?

Answer

Yeah, I did.
Request
잠깐 기다려.
Wait a bit.
Narrative (Talking to self)
오늘 너무 피곤했어.
I was so tired today.

Tone & Feeling

Warm, comfortable, and intimate tone. 해체 is used exclusively among equals and close relationships where formality barriers have been removed. It creates a sense of closeness and familiarity. In text messaging and online communication, -아/어 style dominates as it feels natural and casual. The tone is relaxed and friendly, but should only be used with people you have explicit permission to speak casually with, as using it inappropriately can be seen as extremely disrespectful.
